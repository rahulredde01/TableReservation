Welcome to Mumbai

Mumbai's culinary landscape is a melting pot of flavors, reflecting its diverse culture and heritage. From the iconic street food like vada pav and pav bhaji to coastal delicacies like seafood thalis, the city's cuisine tantalizes taste buds with a blend of spicy, tangy, and savory flavors, showcasing its vibrant culinary heritage.

The Bombay Canteen

Experience the vibrant flavors of Mumbai at The Bombay Canteen, where every dish is a celebration of local ingredients and culinary craftsmanship.

Trishna

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of coastal Indian cuisine at Trishna, a culinary gem in the heart of Mumbai.

Leopold Cafe

Experience the iconic charm of Mumbai at Leopold Cafe, a historic establishment renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and eclectic menu.

Café Mondegar

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Mumbai at Café Mondegar, a beloved hangout spot known for its retro ambiance and hearty comfort food.

Dishkiyaoon

Embark on a culinary journey through modern Indian cuisine at Dishkiyaoon, where innovative dishes and vibrant flavors redefine traditional favorites.

Gajalee

Savor the authentic flavors of coastal Indian cuisine at Gajalee, where fresh seafood and traditional recipes come together to create a memorable experience.

The Earth Plate

Experience a culinary adventure at The Earth Plate, where global flavors come together in a delightful fusion. From Asian stir-fries to Mediterranean delights, each dish is crafted with precision and passion to offer a memorable dining experience that tantalizes your senses and leaves you craving for more.

FUMI - East Asian Spice Trail

Embark on a culinary journey along the East Asian Spice Trail at FUMI, where each dish is a harmonious blend of flavors and spices from the Far East. From aromatic curries to savory stir-fries, FUMI promises to transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of East Asia with every bite.

Ummrao

Experience the royal flavors of Mughlai cuisine at Ummrao, where every dish is a testament to the culinary grandeur of the Mughal era. Indulge in rich curries, aromatic biryanis, and decadent kebabs, meticulously prepared to perfection for a dining experience fit for royalty.

Tanatan Shivaji Park

Immerse yourself in the rich culinary heritage of Indian cuisine at Tanatan Shivaji Park located in mumbai, where each dish is a masterpiece that reflects the timeless traditions of Indian cooking. From succulent kebabs to fragrant biryanis, Tanatan promises a dining experience that celebrates the flavors of India in every bite.

Kebabs & Kurries

Indulge in the exquisite flavors of North Indian cuisine at Kebabs & Kurries, where each dish is a culinary masterpiece crafted with the finest ingredients and authentic spices. From tender kebabs to aromatic curries, Kebabs & Kurries promises a dining experience that delights the senses and transports you to the heart of India.

Nawab Saheb

Embark on a regal culinary journey at Nawab Saheb, where every dish is a tribute to the grandeur of Awadhi cuisine. From fragrant biryanis to succulent kebabs, each bite at Nawab Saheb is a royal experience that transports you to the era of Nawabs and Nizams, promising an unforgettable dining experience.
