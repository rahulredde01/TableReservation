Welcome to Chennai

The food of Chennai, capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant reflection of its coastal location and rich cultural heritage. From aromatic seafood delicacies to traditional South Indian staples like dosas and idlis, Chennai cuisine offers a flavorful blend of spices, flavors, and textures that delight locals and visitors alike.

Anjappar Restaurant

Anjappar Chettinad Restaurant specializes in Chettinad cuisine, known for its spicy and flavorful dishes. From Chicken Chettinad to Fish Curry, it offers a diverse menu.

A2B - Adyar Ananda Bhavan

Adyar Ananda Bhavan, is a popular vegetarian restaurant chain offering a wide range of South Indian snacks, sweets, and meals. With its consistent quality and extensive menu.

Bombay Brasserie

Bombay Brasserie is known for its take on Indian cuisine with modern presentation. From street food-inspired appetizers to contemporary twists on regional specialties.

Kaidi Kitchen

Kaidi Kitchen is a themed restaurant with a unique prison-themed decor and a vegetarian menu inspired by global cuisines. From Indian curries to Chinese stir-fries and Italian pastas.

Coastal Aroma

Coastal Aroma celebrates the coastal flavors of Tamil Nadu with its menu featuring seafood delicacies and traditional dishes from coastal regions. With its fresh ingredients and bold flavors.

Ciclo Cafe

Ciclo Cafe is a unique concept that combines a cycling-themed cafe with a menu featuring healthy and wholesome dishes. From salads and sandwiches to smoothies and artisanal coffee.

The Marina - The Raintree

The Marina-The Raintree hotel, offers menu showcasing the diverse flavors of coastal cuisine. From traditional seafood dishes to fusion creations.

Copper Chimney

Copper Chimney is known for its North Indian cuisine, featuring a selection of tandoori specialties, curries, and biryanis. With its vibrant ambiance.

Sangeetha Restaurant

Sangeetha Vegetarian Restaurant is famous for traditional South Indian vegetarian cuisine .With a menu featuring classics like Masala Dosa, Pongal, and Vada Curry.

Dindigul Thalappakatti

Dindigul Thalappakatti Restaurant is renowned for its Dindigul-style biryanis made with fragrant rice and tender meat cooked to perfection. With its roots in Tamil Nadu's culinary heritage.

Rajdhani Thali Restaurant

Rajdhani Thali Restaurant offers a traditional Rajasthani dining experience with its Thali meals featuring seasonal specialties. From Dal Baati Churma to Gatte Ki Sabzi.

Hotel Junior Kuppanna

Hotel Junior Kuppanna is known for its authentic Chettinad cuisine. With spicy and flavorful dishes prepared with traditional ingredients from Chicken Chettinad to Fish Fry.
