Welcome to Hyderabad

Hyderabad's cuisine is a flavorful blend of Mughlai, Telugu, and Persian influences. Famous for its iconic dish, Hyderabadi biryani, it offers a rich array of kebabs, curries, and desserts. From the aromatic spices of biryani to the tangy flavors of haleem, Hyderabad's food scene is a gastronomic delight.

Paradise Biryani

Paradise is renowned for its authentic Hyderabadi biryani, a flavorful rice dish cooked with marinated meat and aromatic spices.

Ohri's Banjara

Ohri's Banjara is known for its diverse menu featuring Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines. It is from the one of finest restaurants of Hyderabad.

Chutneys

If you're craving South Indian cuisine, Chutneys is the place to go. Known for its delectable array of dosas, idlis, and vadas served with an assortment of chutneys.

Minerva Coffee Shop

Minerva Coffee Shop is famous for traditional Andhra cuisine. From spicy curries to crispy dosas, this restaurant offers a wide range of dishes bursting with authentic flavors.

Bawarchi

Bawarchi is known for its biryani prepared with fragrant Basmati rice and tender meat cooked to perfection. Located in the bustling streets of Hyderabad.

Mehfil

The Mehfil Restaurant Known for its Mughlai and blend of North Indian cuisine. Mehfil offers a regal dining experience with its elegant decor and live music performances.

Rayalaseema Ruchulu

Rayalaseema Ruchulu showcases the flavors of the Rayalaseema region. known for its spicy and flavorful dishes such as Gongura Mutton and Natu Kodi Pulusu.

Absolute Barbecue (AB's)

This restaurant offers a unique dining experience with its interactive grilling tables where guests can grill their own skewers of meats, seafood, and vegetables.

Nizam's Club

Step back in time and dine like royalty at Nizam's Club, a historic establishment that has been serving traditional Hyderabadi cuisine for decades.

Cafe Bahar

Cafe Bahar is known for its flavorful biryanis, kebabs, and tandoori delights served in generous portions, making it a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Farzi Cafe

Farzi Cafe is known for its innovative and playful take on classic dishes like Molecular Jalebi Caviar, Chicken Tikka Masala Tacos, and Dal Chawal Arancini.

Kritunga Restaurant

Kritunga is known for its Andhra cuisine. From spicy curries to crispy fried snacks, Kritunga serves up authentic Andhra flavors in a casual dining setting.
