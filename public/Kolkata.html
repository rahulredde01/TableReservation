Welcome to Kolkata

Kolkata's food tradition is a vibrant tapestry of flavors, blending influences from Bengali, Mughlai, and British cuisines. From aromatic biryanis to delicate Sandesh sweets, and hearty fish curries to spicy street food delights, Kolkata's culinary landscape is a celebration of diversity, heritage, and unmatched gastronomic experiences.

Kewpie's Kitchen

Kewpie's Kitchen is a renowned eatery in Kolkata known for its authentic Bengali cuisine. It offers a variety of traditional dishes like Shorshe Ilish and Chingri Malai Curry.

Oh! Calcutta

Oh! Calcutta is known for its contemporary take on Bengali cuisine that celebrates the flavors of Kolkata. Dishes like Aloo Postovand Bhetki Paturi showcase the region's culinary heritage.

Arsalan

Arsalan is famous for Mughlai cuisine, particularly biryanis and kebabs. It's a favorite among locals for indulging in rich and flavorful dishes like Chicken Chaap and Mutton Rezala.

Bhojohori Manna

Bhojohori Manna is a popular restaurant offering traditional Bengali delicacies. From Maacher Paturi to Chitol Maacher Muitha. It's a heaven for lovers of authentic Bengali flavors.

Bohemian

Bohemian is a contemporary restaurant that blends Bengali flavors with global culinary influences. With its innovative dishes like Bhetki Paturi Ravioli and Kosha Mangsho Tacos.

Kasturi Restaurant

Kasturi Restaurant is a popular destination for traditional Bengali thalis in Kolkata. With its extensive menu featuring a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.

Calcutta Club

Calcutta Club is a heritage restaurant known for its colonial-era ambiance and traditional Bengali fare. From authentic seafood dishes to hearty meat curries.

Boishakhi Restaurant

Boishakhi Restaurant is a long-standing establishment, celebrated for its traditional Bengali thalis. With its commitment to authentic flavors and generous portions.

Suruchi Restaurant

Suruchi Restaurant is a longstanding authentic vegetarian Bengali restaurant. Serving a wide array of traditional dishes like Bhaja, Dal, Bharta, and more. It offers a wholesome dining experience.

Bhojohori Manna

Bhojohori Manna is a renowned kolkata's culinary, offering authentic Bengali cuisine. It serves up iconic dishes like Prawn Malai Curry and Chitol Muitha.

Kolkata Biryani House

Kolkata Biryani House is a popular restaurant specializing in Kolkata-style biryanis. They offer a variety of biryanis including Chicken, Mutton, and Egg.

Saptapadi

Saptapadi is a Bengali restaurant known for its delicious meals. From Bhaja to Bharta and Bhapa, it serves up a variety of classic Bengali dishes with authentic flavors.
