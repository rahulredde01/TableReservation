Welcome to Delhi

Delhi's cuisine is a diverse tapestry woven with flavors from across India. From rich Mughlai delicacies like butter chicken and kebabs to spicy street food delights like chaat and parathas, Delhi offers a culinary journey through its bustling markets and historic lanes, satisfying every palate with its vibrant gastronomy.

Karim's

Karim's is a legendary restaurant known for its Mughlai cuisine. It serves iconic dishes such as Mutton Korma, Seekh Kebabs, and Chicken Jahangiri in setting near Jama Masjid.

Indian Accent

Indian Accent is an restaurant offering innovative Indian cuisine with a modern twist. It presents dishes like Duck Khurchan and Tamarind Margarita in an elegant ambiance.

Bukhara

Bukhara is a culinary institution renowned for its rustic North Indian fare and iconic dishes like Dal Bukhara and Sikandari Raan. It provides a timeless dining experience.

Punjab Grill

Punjab Grill is known for its contemporary traditional dishes blended with modern aroma of Punjabi cuisine, featuring dishes like Tandoori Guchchi and Galouti Kebabs.

Gulati Restaurant

Gulati Restaurant is a long-standing favorite among Delhiites for its delicious North Indian and Mughlai cuisine like Butter Chicken and Rogan Josh in a bustling atmosphere.

Jamavar - The Leela Palace

Jamavar is an upscale restaurant in The Leela Palace offering authentic flavors of North and South India. It serves dishes like Malabar Fish Curry and Murgh Khatta Pyaaz.

Saravana Bhavan

Saravana Bhavan is a popular South Indian vegetarian restaurant. It serves classics like Masala Dosa, Sambar, and Filter Coffee in a casual setting.

Pind Balluchi

Pind Balluchi is a rustic-themed restaurant that celebrates the flavors of Punjab with dishes like Sarson Ka Saag, Makki Ki Roti, and Amritsari Fish.

Carnatic Cafe

Carnatic Cafe is a cozy South Indian restaurant known for its traditional flavors and homely ambiance. It serves a variety of dosas, idlis, vadas, and filter coffee.

Naturals Ice Cream

Naturals Ice Cream is known for its delicious and natural ice creams. With unique flavors like Tender Coconut and Alphonso Mango.

Wenger's Deli

Wenger's Deli is a legendary bakery in Connaught Place, known for its freshly baked bread, cakes, pastries, and sandwiches. With its delectable offerings.

Barbeque Nation

Barbeque Nation is a unique dining experience with its buffet style barbecue where guests can grill their own skewers of meats, seafood, and vegetables.
